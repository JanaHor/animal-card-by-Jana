Lion

A lion may sleep up to 20 hours a day.

  • Genus and Species: Panthera leo
  • Size: up to 3 m long + 60-91 cm long tail
  • Weight: 150 - 250 kg
  • Native habitat: Africa, India

Lions are large felines that are traditionally depicted as the "king of the jungle." Male lions are generally larger than females and have a distinctive mane of hair around their heads. The mane's function is to make the male look more impressive to females and more intimidating to other males. The mane also protects the male's neck during fights over territory or mating rights.
